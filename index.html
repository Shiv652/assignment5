Professional Experience


I am a Front end design PG student at George Brown College, recently graduated as a UX Designer from St. Lawrence College. I have worked as a UI designer before that for 5 years after completing my graduation in Computer Science Engineering from Chitkara University, Baddi. Art has always been my hobby therefore I gave a shot at making it a part of my career. I have been working on various design content consistently for over 5 years. Have amassed knowledge and skills in Photoshop, Illustrator, XD, Figma, and a few video editing software. My thought process is more along the lines of easy-to-grasp and lazy perspective, which is the millennial's choice. Minimalism is in, so I don't go overboard with my work, however, it depends on the viewer's choice.
